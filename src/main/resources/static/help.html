üí° Search Tips

  • ‚úÖ
    Use Natural Language
    Write your query like you're asking a friend. Instead of "love faith hope", try "what does the Bible say about faith, hope, and love?"
  • ‚úÖ
    Describe Concepts
    Search for ideas and themes rather than specific words. "How to handle difficult people" will find relevant verses even without exact matches.
  • ‚úÖ
    Try Different Phrasings
    If you don't get the results you want, rephrase your query. "God's love" and "how much does God love me" may give different results.
  • ‚úÖ
    Adjust the Score Threshold
    Lower the Min Score to see more results (less relevant), or raise it to see only highly relevant verses.
  • ‚úÖ
    Filter by Version
    Use the version filter to search only in English (ASV) or Korean (KRV) if you want results in a specific language.
  • ‚úÖ
    Click to Read More
    Click on any search result to open the full chapter and read the verse in context.
